Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Numra for?
Numra is designed for mid-market and enterprise finance teams with meaningful transaction volume, recurring workflows, and established processes. Smaller businesses with lighter workloads generally won’t see enough benefit from an agent.
What types of workflows are a good fit for an agent?
If your team repeats it, we can usually automate it. Agents handle both the predictable steps and the messy real-world logic that other tools struggle with.
How much of our process needs to change to use Numra?
In most cases, very little. Agents are built around how your process works today. As we map it, we often spot steps that can be removed or simplified, but only where the outcome stays exactly the same.
How long does it take to get an agent live?
It depends on the workflow, but most agents are built, tested and deployed within weeks.
What systems can you integrate with?
If a system has an API, we can usually connect to it. Integrations are scoped during discovery.
How do you ensure accuracy and control?
Agents run inside a controlled environment with human-in-the-loop steps where required. Nothing is posted without passing your defined checks.
What happens when our processes change?
Minor tweaks are included in support. Bigger process changes require new build work, similar to retraining a team when the workflow shifts. The agent is then updated to follow the new way of working.
What if we try this and the ROI isn’t there?
We size the ROI before you commit. If it’s not there, we don’t move forward. If you proceed, the build is backed by a 12-month payback guarantee.
